RAG rating examples

To help you choose a rating, we've provided some examples of what you might include when RAG (red, amber, green) rating your projects.

The examples do not cover every scenario and are simply to help you decide how to rate a project.

Spend risk

Green

No rising costs, no slippage, less than 30% expenditure profiled in final year, no use of financial flexibility.

Amber/Green

Project progressing but with minor issues, issues are within control and there is a mitigation plan in place, there is no additional support required.

Amber

Cost increases of 10% to 20% which are manageable, a funding gap exists but a source has been identified (although not secured), there is slippage of 20% to 30% between financial years, 40% to 50% of expenditure is profiled in the final year, the use of flexibility is required but below the threshold.

Amber/Red

No rising costs, no slippage, less than 30% expenditure profiled in final year, no use of financial flexibility.

Red

No rising costs, no slippage, less than 30% expenditure profiled in final year, no use of financial flexibility.
